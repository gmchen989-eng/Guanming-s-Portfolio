National Youth Achievement Award (Bronze)

Date: October 2020

The National Youth Achievement Award (NYAA) Bronze is a prestigious youth development program in Singapore that encourages secondary school students to develop essential life skills and personal qualities. To achieve the Silver Award, participants engage in activities across four key areas: community service, adventurous journeys, skill development, and physical recreation. These activities promote self-discipline, perseverance, and civic responsibility. The Bronze Award, administered by the NYAA Council, is a significant milestone that recognizes the dedication and accomplishments of young people striving for personal and social excellence.
